Hourly

Since: June 2022

I have many passions, and I like to keep track of how much time I dedicate to each of them. Hourly simply combines time-tracking functionality with a to-do list interface for topic-related tasks. It also has a feature in which you can add time to selected areas of activity. Hourly holds me accountable to my commitments while also giving me a sense of affirmation for the work I've already done. Basically, I wanted to make something Malcolm Gladwell would appreciate.

What I Learned:

  • Working with the MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, and Node) tech stack
  • Working with dates and times in javascript (harder than you might think!)
